Our Work

Developing products, programs and applications to inspire and improve.

Animal Heroes

In partnership with Mystic Aquarium, we developed Animal Heroes, a subscription-based multimedia program that brings wildlife, nature and science education to life for kids 6–11 and includes games, quizzes, activities, and AR.

ZIPPRD

ZIPPRD is a digital marketplace and product efficacy platform for edtech entrepreneurs to present, test, pilot, iterate, and commercialize their edtech products to schools and organizations in a connected centralized community.

ARx

In collaboration with Connecticut Children’s, we are developing ARx, the only Health Literacy tool that uses mixed reality content and storytelling to better present and bring health information to life for old/young patients.
