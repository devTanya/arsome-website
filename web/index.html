Our Work

Building Digital Projects & Products

Animal Heroes

In partnership with Mystic Aquarium, ARSOME developed & commercialized Animal Heroes, a subscription-based multimedia program that brings nature and science to life for kids 6–11 with augmented reality.

VR Driving Simulator

Our VR Driving Simulator is the most realistic driving simulator available in the market. It's an ideal training tool for first responders, police, military, driving schools, and companies that drive products to customers.

AR Health Literacy

Most patients don't understand medical instructions, medical reports, or prescription information. This lack of health literacy costs our economy $200 billion annually. ARx is a platform to increase a patient's literacy.

Giving Arsome

From collecting cans to raise money for Spotty Dog Rescue, a CT non-profit dedicated to helping stray dogs find loving homes, to volunteering as a start-up advisor for CTNext, we are dedicated to improving our community.
