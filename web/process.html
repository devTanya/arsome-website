Process

Our project management process is unique, managed by a Certified Project Manager, and tailored to the time frame and budget of the client. All code and design are 100% American-made by talented in-house designers, developers and innovators. Satisfaction is our #1 priority. All information obtained is confidential and key technical requirements are provided and documented.

Storyboard

Storyboard of an AR mobile application for Pfizer

Every project starts with a storyboard. We solidify the project and technology requirements, client expectations and map out the delivery schedule. We analyze motivations of the end user and recommend strategies for engagement and connectivity. We define each deliverable with descriptions, deadlines, and activities to ensure all stakeholders are aligned.

Developing experiences for clients and partners.

Continuous improvement and iteration are the building blocks to a successful development phase. After the storyboard is solidified, we begin coding the software and system requirements. Testing

Testing VR CDL driver training software & hardware.

Following the development phase, we identify a strategy to gain user feedback in a controlled pilot study. It's designed to validate the value proposition of the project like higher conversion, better promotion, or more effective learning. Pilot users can remit feedback in a controlled and autonomous way. We obtain the pilot data, develop trends and present the results to stakeholders for strategic assessment.

Deploying an LMS of Animal Heroes for Mystic Aquarium.

We wrap the project up by providing a Maintenance Management Plan, describing the deployment risks, contingency and corrective measures for the software.
